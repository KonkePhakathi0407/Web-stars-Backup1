Mental Health Tips

Knowledge Is Power

Learn about mental health and how to support yourself and others

🧘

Tips for Coping with Stress

  • Practice deep breathing for 5 minutes daily
  • Break big tasks into small steps
  • Take regular breaks (Pomodoro method)
  • Limit caffeine and social media
  • Talk to a friend or counsellor
🌱

Daily Mental Health Habits

  • 8 hours of quality sleep
  • 30 minutes of physical activity
  • Write 3 things you're grateful for
  • Connect with someone you trust
  • Spend time in nature
⚠️

Signs You May Need Help

  • Persistent sadness or hopelessness
  • Withdrawal from friends and activities
  • Changes in sleep or appetite
  • Difficulty concentrating
  • Feeling overwhelmed consistently
📚

Student-Specific Resources

  • Campus wellness centres
  • Peer support groups on campus
  • Online therapy platforms (low cost)
  • Student mental health apps
  • University counselling services
